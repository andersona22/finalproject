Unswayed

For people who want to know the actual story on a topic

Open Web App

Unswayed is an app that will allow you to look up current topics that are happening in the world and give you it without bias opinions like the ones you would hear on the news. This allows you to created your own opinion on a certain topic without someone telling you what to think




Think:
Think of a particular topic that you have heard on the news that you want to know more about.
Search:
Search for that particular topic on the app and read about the topic so you can get more knowledge on what the topic is about without bias opinions.
Learn:
Learn about the particular topics and create your own opinion on the topic since you have now read the information without any bias views.

Design Process

How this app has been designed and developed
I asked my boyfriend Connor the following questions:

What do you think social fabric means?
What holds a society together. Such as culture, religion, government, beliefs, and morals.

When thinking about something more specific like education, how does social fabric have to do with that?
We as a country are a very educated group, so we require everyone to go to school to get an education, to further grow our society/ country.

Do you think we can strenghten the social fabric involving education with media?
Yes, but we can not be bias. Which will never happen in todays society. But people grow up hearing the media and then they are being educated on the bias opinions. If we want to improve, we need to have people think for themselves and not just repeat what they hear like puppets.

If you were creating an app to fix this situation, what would it be?
An app that is both liberal and conservative so that both sides can be heard and the media isn't changing the story to make it fit into their agenda or narritve.
